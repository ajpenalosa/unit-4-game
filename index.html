Crystals Collector

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Each of the crystals below has a hidden value. By clicking on a crystal, that crystal's value will be added to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score exactly with the random number. You lose the game if you go over the random number.

Each time a new game starts, you will be given a new random number and each of the crystals will have a new hidden value.

Good luck!
